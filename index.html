My Story

I've spent the majority of my professional career pursuing forestry, arboriculture, and ecological restoration across the United States with the hopes of leaving this planet in better shape than I found it. Along the way I studied song birds in the south, fixed bicycles in the west, built cell towers in the north, and restored historic timber frames in the east. The journey has been incredible but I realized I could do more for the planet through technology than through manual labor. This lead to a radical shift towards programming which, combined with my previous experience, has given me adaptability and an unique perspective towards the challanges ahead.

Bootcamp Projects

Hoop Hype

HoopHype is a quick mobile app designed to to help you choose a live basketball game to go see. Using an algorithm to compare your chosen team to all of their opponents, HoopHype ranks the games in the season so you know what'll be a game worth seeing.

Word Guess

Word Guess, another classic Javascript game, also an alternative to hangman for those of you mindful of the politically correct. The computer has a word in mind; try to spell out the word using on the on-screen keyboard within the alotted number of guesses. Otherwise the game restarts with a new word!

Crystal Collector

Crystal Collector, a classic JS game using basic logic to guess what number the computer is thinking. Clicking on images of crystals presents random numbers you must combine to match the computers number in mind.

Spor

Spor is a full stack mushroom identification applicaton. Users can log in to their 'basket' where they'll have access to an identification system utilizing stacked mushroom features to narrow down their search, a mushroom facts page for each mushroom in the database, the ability to add found mushrooms to their basket which takes a snap shot of the date and geo location when the found button was pressed, and an interective map where they'll see every found mushroom pinned to the standard google map.

Bench Strength

Bench Strength is a full stack Angular app that gives senior developers, project managers, administrative users, etc, the ability to view their entire teams strengths and interests in terms of programming languages and technolgies. Basic users will log in to their profile where they can build up their personal card of skills and interests along with links to github projects that demostrate those skills. Administrative users will have the ability to add a realistic skill level for each skill created on a users page. They'll be able to build teams for projects down the pipeline tailored towards skill level and interest.
