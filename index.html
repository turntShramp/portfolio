My Story

I've spent the majority of my professional career pursuing forestry, arboriculture, and ecological restoration. Along the way I studied song birds in the south, fixed bicycles in the west, built cell towers in the north, and restored historic timber frames in the east. The journey has been incredible but I realized I could do more for the planet through technology than through manual labor. This lead to a radical shift towards programming which, combined with my previous experience, makes me a super hero. There's no limit to what you're capapble of with effort and determination.
