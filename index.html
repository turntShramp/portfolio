Sagan Mín

I've spent the majority of my professional career pursuing forestry, arboriculture, and ecological restoration across the United States with the hopes of leaving this planet in better shape than I found it. Along the way I studied song birds in the south, fixed bicycles in the west, built cell towers in the north, and restored historic timber frames in the east. Bootcamp Projects

Hoop Hype

HoopHype is a quick mobile app designed to to help you choose a live basketball game to go see. Using an algorithem to compare your chosen team to all of their opponents, HoopHype ranks the games in the season so you know what'll be a game worth seeing.

Word Guess

Word Guess, another classic Javascript game, also an alternative to hangman for those of you mindful of the politically correct. The computer has a word in mind; try to spell out the word using on the on-screen keyboard within the alotted number of guesses. Otherwise the game restarts with a new word!

Crystal Collector

Crystal Collector, a classic JS game using basic logic to guess what number the computer is thinking. Clicking on images of crystals presents random numbers you must combine to match the computers number in mind.
